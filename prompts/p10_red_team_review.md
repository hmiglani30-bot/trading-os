# P10 — Red-Team Research Review

## Purpose

Challenge a completed memo or report. Identify weak evidence, hidden assumptions, alternative explanations, correlation risk, timing risk, and reasons to delay.

## Required inputs

- Completed memo or report.
- User time horizon.
- Portfolio context if available.
- Market regime context if available.

## Output format

```markdown
# Red-Team Review — <Ticker/Theme>

## 1. Strongest Objections
| Objection | Why it matters | Evidence needed | Severity |
|---|---|---|---|

## 2. Hidden Assumptions

## 3. Missing Data That Could Change the View

## 4. Alternative Explanations

## 5. Portfolio and Correlation Risk

## 6. Timing and Event Risk

## 7. Narrative Trap Check
Is the idea driven by price movement, social proof, recent headlines, or fear of missing out?

## 8. Conditions for Downgrading Confidence

## 9. Revised Confidence Score

## 10. Follow-Up Checklist
```

## Quality checks

- Be adversarial but fair.
- Do not invent objections unsupported by facts.
- Focus on reasoning quality, not outcome prediction.
- Explicitly identify whether waiting for better data would improve process quality.
